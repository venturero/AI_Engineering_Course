Autonomous driving commercialization is bifurcating across capability tiers.
Level 2+ and Level 3 systems are scaling through premium vehicle segments and fleet use cases, while Level 4 robotaxi deployment remains geographically narrow and regulation-sensitive.
Industry participants report that software update cadence, validation datasets, and compute cost per mile are now stronger determinants of moat than standalone sensor novelty.
OEM partnerships and cloud-infrastructure alliances are accelerating deployment timelines but also increasing platform dependence.
